The Boston Vegan was created by vegans for vegans to highlight some of the tastiest restaurants around Boston that offer a wide array of vegan items.

Of course, this eater's guide is meant to be used by everyone. Our hope is to share the yummiest finds both so that vegans may have a stress-free culinary exploration experience and so that non-vegans may realize that a plant-based diet does not mean sacrificing flavor!

This website is brought to you by Klara and Dorina who have spent the last years exploring and sampling their way through the greater-Boston area to bring you a compilation of their favorite finds and the opportunity for you to add your opinions to that list! We hope you enjoy :)
